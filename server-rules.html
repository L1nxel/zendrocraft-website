Server Rules

Please follow these rules to keep ZendroCraft fun and fair for everyone.

1

No Griefing or Stealing

Do not destroy, steal, or tamper with other players’ builds, items, or farms without permission.

2

No Hacking, Cheats, or Exploits

Using hacked clients, x-ray, dupes, or any form of cheating is forbidden.

Normal farms (such as iron farms, villager farms, etc.) are allowed. Ask staff if you're unsure whether something counts as exploiting.

3

PvP Rules

PvP is only allowed inside designated arenas (when available).

4

Build Respectfully

You may build near other players, but do not build directly on top of or below someone else’s base. Keep at least 5 blocks of space between builds.

5

No Advertising Other Servers

Do not advertise other Minecraft servers in chat.

6

Use Common Sense

If something feels like it ruins the fun or experience for others, it probably isn’t allowed.

Breaking these rules may result in a warning, temporary ban, or permanent ban depending on severity.

When in doubt, ask a staff member!
